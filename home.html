Intro:

Batman Beyond began airing on January 10th, 1999 and lasted three seasons. Airing its finale on December 18, 2001.

A spin off of Batman the animated series, Batman Beyond focuses on Terry McGinnis, a teenager in 2040, who fights crime as the new Batman for Neo Gotham. Under the stern supervision of an elderly Bruce Wayne, he fights crime in a harsh, futuristic Gotham. The show was created by Paul Dini, Bruce Tim and Alan Burnett. Many cast members from across the DC animated tv line-up return to voice older versions of their beloved characters, such as Kevin Conroy as Bruce Wayne.

Characters

Terry McGinnis/Batman

Terry McGinnis is 17 years of age when he becomes the new Batman of Neo Gotham. He takes up the mantle to avenge the death of his father, but continues on as Neo Gothams dark knight as there will always be a need for a Batman in Gotham. Bruce trains Terry and helps him through Terrys earpiece. They make an effective crime fighting team. Terry grew up in rough neighbourhoods, due to Batman not being around for a lot of his youth. This gave Terry adequate street fighting skills e=which he utilised as Batman along with his many gadgets such as batarangs, grappling gun etc.

Bruce Wayne

Bruce Wayne, the original Batman, has retired. After almost resorting to using a gun, breaking his code, Bruce retires from his role as the dark crusader. He becomes a recluse in Wayne manor as he lets the world go on without him. One day he saves a young Terry McGinnis from a mugging outside Wayne manor, changing his life for the better. Bruce begins to reluctantly train Terry to become the new Batman. Using the Batcomputer and his extensive knowledge of Gotham and its rogues, Bruce helps Terry become the greatest Batman he can be.

Derek Powers/Blight

Derek Powers is a ruthless business man and a violent villain. He's the owner of Powers Technology, which merged with Wayne Enterprises many years ago. After being exposed to a biological weapon he designed Derek is treated to radiation to counteract the effects, this turns him into the supervillain and archenemy of Batman, Blight. Blight has the power of radiokinesis which he uses against Batman. Blight seemigly drowns in a submerine battle with the Batman. However his body is never recovered, leaving Batman to believe that he may still be around.




Legacy

From its very nature as a show set in the future of an already established universe, Batman Beyond heavily deals with legacy.


Terry takes on the role of Batman and all that comes with it whether he realises that at first or not. Terry has the luxury of being trained in by his predecessor, yet at times struggles with what it means to be Batman. Terry is not Bruce and finds it difficult at times to see himself as the caped crusader because of it. Yet, he makes the mantle his own, when I was growing up Terry was the Batman I watched, similar to how it must be for the children of Neo Gotham, who never saw Bruce's Batman. Batman becomes more of a myth than a figure and Terry becomes this myth, this symbol of fear for villains and a symbol of hope and justice for the average civilian.

Terry later meets the Justice League of his time, faces the Joker reborn and a host of classic characters. Some take time to accept him as Batman and some accept him immediately. But its important to know that they all accept him in the role. Legacy is taking an inheritance and doing your best with it. Terry takes the mantle of the dark knight and does so much good with it that its difficult to say he isn't Batman.

Terry isn't Bruce, yet he is vengeance, he is the night, he is... Batman.
