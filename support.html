Support Page

We compiled a number of frequently asked questions about this site, in the hopes of assisting people who may be asking these questions later. If you have anymore questions, please use the submission box below or contact us through our contact page. Thank you.

•Question: How many people run this site?
•Answer: Its just me at the moment.

•Question: Whats your favourite episode?
•Answer: My favourite episode is "Meltdown". It deals with the classic Mr Freeze in 2040, it acts as a fitting end for the well written character.

•Question: What sparked your interest in Batman Beyond?/What do you like about the show?
•Answer: I watched the show when I was a kid and fell in love with it. I always liked how the show dealt with legacy and going beyond the status quo that the DC universe so often falls back on, its also just a really good show!

•Question: How do I enter the monthly competition?
•Answer: All information about entering the monthly competition is available on the competitions page.
